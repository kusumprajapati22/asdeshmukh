We, at Kirtane & Pandit, believe in the value of optimizing risks and returns to enhance the strength of a company. Our internal audit assurance services and risk management solutions are designed to achieve this objective effectively.


As proactive consultants, we no longer limit ourselves to traditional risk-based internal auditing. Our approach has evolved to encompass much more than that. We focus on various aspects, including internal audit assurance, statutory compliances, prevention of revenue leakages, and providing valuable suggestions to identify cost reduction and cost control areas.




With our expertise in internal audits and risk management, we aim to add value to your organization by protecting your assets, improving efficiency, and helping you make informed business decisions.

Kirtane & Pandit LLP Chartered Accountants, Chartered Accountants is an Accounting, Auditing & Consulting firm with a well-established network of financial experts across India.
