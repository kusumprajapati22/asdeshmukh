At Kirtane & Pandit, we specialize in delivering high-quality accounting and compliance services. Our experienced team of chartered accountants and professionals ensures the integrity and accuracy of financial information through impeccable statutory audits. We offer a wide range of services, including audits under the Income Tax Act, annual reviews of US GAAP/IFRS adjustments, advisory and implementation support for transition to IND-AS, IFRS, and US GAAP, due diligence and valuation services, and more. With our meticulous approach, innovative methodologies, and value-adding services, we strive to exceed client expectations and provide valuable insights for your business.


Kirtane & Pandit LLP Chartered Accountants, Chartered Accountants is an Accounting, Auditing & Consulting firm with a well-established network of financial experts across India.
