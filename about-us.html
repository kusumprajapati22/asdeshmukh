FCA, NET, M.COM, FAFD (Forensic Audit ICAI).
Mr. Amol Sanjay Deshmukh, is a Post-Graduate and a Chartered Accountant in practice since 2011 in Pune. He has been proficient in Statutory Audit and Assurance, Direct Taxation, Internal Audit and Goods and Service tax (GST) related compliances of Various Sugar factories, and Distillery units, agro processing units, Manufacturing, Services Sector, Educational Institutions, Traders, Dealers, Builders and Developers, Sugar related technology industry firms, operating as a Co Operatives Societies, trust, Public or Private Companies, Partnership Firms, LLPs, Proprietary Concerns, Housing Co Operative Society, Association of Person and Body of Individuals.

He had exposure to Work with S.R. Batliboi & CO. member firm Ernst and Young (EY) a Multinational Auditing Entity where he got exposure to work on Audits of various multinational entities like Suzlon Energy Limited, Bilcare Limited, Sterlite Limited, Honeywell Automation Limited, Alfa lavel Limited. Besides he work on Special Assignment of Kumar Builders & Developers to make IFRS Complied Financial Statement.

Besides he had active participation in the Statutory Audit of Affinity Express India Private Limited, General Advertising India Private Limited member companies of USA based Affinity Express Group while working with Ernst and Young (EY).

FCA A.S. Deshmukh also has achievement of appearing for Interview of IAS exam conducted by Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) in 2014 to his credit. Where experienced and Knowledge gained enables him to understand and solved various intricacies of Industrial World.

FCA Amol S. Deshmukh has cleared Forensic Auditor examination conducted by Institute of Chartered Accountant of India (ICAI), New Delhi, therefore presently he is part of “Forensic Accounting and Fraud Detection” FAFD where he has potential to explore new and emerging field of Forensic Audits and Fraud detection.

FCA Amol S. Deshmukh has recently completed Certificate Course on Artificial Intelligence for CA (AICA)-Level 1 which is recent exploration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in auditing in order to provide high value added services to clients.

Presently A. S. Firm based on the experience of working with small unit of sugar to the largest sugar plant with spectrum of wide services to sugar industry ensure value addition to clients business.
