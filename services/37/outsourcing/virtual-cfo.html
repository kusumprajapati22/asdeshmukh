At Kirtane & Pandit, our virtual CFO services provide outsourced assistance in managing the financial requirements of your organization, similar to the role of a chief financial officer in larger companies. Our experienced financial professionals act as trusted advisors, offering expertise in financial planning, budgeting, cash flow management, and strategic decision-making.


We are committed to delivering services with integrity and accountability, following international standards, independence, and ethical guidelines set by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).


Our strong reputation has secured us a spot on the top tier of the empanelled firm list by the Comptroller and Auditor General (C & AG) of India and the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). This distinction allows us to audit large government undertakings and banks, and we have been appointed by RBI and government undertakings for numerous large and special investigative assignments. Additionally, we are a registered firm with the Public Company Accounting Oversight Board (PCAOB), making us eligible to work with companies listed on stock exchanges in the USA.


We provide flexible engagement models to suit:

1. Advisory:

a. Structuring of Cash Flows and Balance Sheet

b. Designing MIS dashboards & reporting

c. Treasury Management / Working capital management

d. Transaction advisory

e. Drawing Budgets and Controls

f. Automation and ease of processes

g. Accounting and financial policies


2. Monitoring:

a. Day-to-day accounting

b. Preparing MIS dashboards and reports

c. Tracking Budgets / Variance analysis

d. Finalization of accounts

e. Ensuring audit preparedness

f. Banking relationship management

g. Review of vendor/customer agreements and contracts


3. Compliance:

a. Tax - Direct and Indirect

b. Secretarial

c. Labour Laws
