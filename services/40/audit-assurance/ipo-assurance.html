Our IPO and Capital Market Assurance services provide comprehensive audit and assurance solutions to support companies navigating the public listing process in India. We specialize in delivering pre-IPO audits, financial due diligence, and ensuring compliance with SEBI and stock exchange requirements. Our services include comfort letters, financial statement certifications, and independent assurance for prospectus disclosures. With a focus on accuracy, transparency, and regulatory adherence, we enable businesses to inspire investor confidence and achieve seamless access to capital markets.
